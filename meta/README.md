## Tips

- Meta focuses on ultra-high-scale, low-latency, globally distributed systems
- Be prepared to discuss trade-offs (latency vs consistency vs cost)
- Practice back-of-the-envelope capacity estimates
- Know real-time systems, personalization pipelines, and multi-region replication
- Focus on clear assumptions, measurable decisions, and operational concerns
